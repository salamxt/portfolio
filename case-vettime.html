The Challenge

The 11 PM Phone Call

The client called at 11 PM. His cat had stopped eating, and he'd spent 45 minutes trying to find an emergency vet online.

"Every website wanted me to browse. I didn't want to browse. I wanted someone to tell me where to go."

ğŸ’¡ Key Insight

Users in emergency mode aren't rational decision-makers. They don't want fifty choices. They want rescue.

The Solution

Three Taps to Help

We tested showing all 50 nearby clinics vs auto-filtering to just 3 options. The limited choice version had 3x higher completion rates.

"I don't care which clinic is 4.8 vs 4.9 stars. I care that my dog is throwing up and someone can see him in 20 minutes."
